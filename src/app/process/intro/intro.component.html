From the moment my blades first touched the ice,
I've been obsessed with hockey.

As the statistical analyst for UNC Hockey, I developed a passion for identifying the nuances of the sport and measuring their impact on team success through game data.

Driven either by jealousy or brazenness, I decided to challenge my understanding of hockey and knowledge of statistics against the best in the world, Sportsbook Oddsmakers.

I wanted to know if I could predict the outcome of NHL games better than the people who make a living off of it. I wanted to know if I could find flaws in their models and exploit them. I wanted to know if I could beat the house.

This project is my endeavor to achieve that. I'll employ machine learning algorithms to forecast NHL game outcomes and juxtapose my predictions with those of Sportsbooks. Analyzing the data, I aim to unearth trends for gaining an edge against the house.
